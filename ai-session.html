Hands-on Training Session

Working with AI,
hands on.

Facilitated by Dr. Fatime Barbara Hegyi Audience: The team — all levels Format: Interactive · ≈ 2h15

The programme

00 Opening

A short talk to open minds

Set the tone: curiosity over fear, judgement over hype.

A short presentation and a few inspiring words to open the room and set the tone for the day.

10 minTakeaway: we're here to do, not watch
★ Warm-up · on your feet

Where do we stand?

Get people moving — and read the room's real starting point.

Read out a few statements and everyone moves to where they stand. Instant energy — and an honest sense of where the room really is.

5 minTakeaway: an honest baseline
01 Activity · quiz

How wrong are we about AI?

Bust the myths before we build the skills.

A playful quiz on what everyone gets wrong. Reveal the answers, then talk them through together.

10 minTakeaway: learning and curiosity
02 Activity · teams

Prompt battle

Discover that the prompt — not the tool — does the work.

Same task for every team: craft the best prompt for a short speech and a matching presentation. Five minutes to write it. Then each team presents its result live — and the funniest one wins.

15 minTakeaway: better input → better output
03 Activity · together

Make the prompt better, together

Learn the anatomy of a strong prompt — live.

Improve one prompt as a group, layer by layer: role, context, audience, format, constraints, examples. Re-run after each addition so everyone watches the output transform.

15 minTakeaway: a repeatable recipe
04 Activity · detective work

Spot the hallucination

See how confident — and how wrong — AI can be at once.

Hand out a polished AI answer seeded with a fake statistic, a fake citation, and a plausible-but-wrong fact. Teams hunt for the errors. The shock of how believable it looks is the point.

10 minTakeaway: a human must verify
05 Activity · red team

Break it

Build healthy scepticism by finding the cracks.

Teams get a few minutes to make the AI fail: contradict itself, fumble a simple count, miss a basic fact. Where it stumbles is exactly what we need to see.

10 minTakeaway: know where it's weak
06 Activity · game

AI or human?

Calibrate your eye for what AI output really looks like.

Show short snippets — a paragraph, a caption, maybe an image — and the room guesses which is AI-made. Quick, competitive, and humbling. It tunes everyone's intuition.

10 minTakeaway: a sharper, sceptical eye
07 Activity · inspiration

A menu of use cases

See the breadth — then find what fits your work.

Walk through real, everyday ways AI helps — drafting, summarising, data, prep, and more.

10 minTakeaway: a personal shortlist
08 Activity · your work

A recurring task, solved

Make the payoff personal and immediate.

Everyone picks one recurring task from their own week. We choose a few and work through together how to tackle them with AI.

20 minTakeaway: real use cases, today
09 Activity · end to end

From data to a presentation

See the full workflow: messy input → insight → something shareable.

Use AI to analyse a dataset and turn it into a clear, presentable summary. A great moment to insist, one last time, on checking the numbers.

20 minTakeaway: a thinking partner, verified

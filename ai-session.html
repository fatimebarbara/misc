Hands-on Training Session · Programme

Working with AI,
hands on.

We don't learn AI by hearing about it. We learn it by using it — a few hours to play, to test, to catch where it fails, and to leave a little braver and more capable than we walked in.

Facilitated by Fatime Barbara Audience: The unit — all levels Format: Interactive · ≈ 2 hours

The programme

Framing · we set the scene Together · we think as a group Hands-on · we play & build
00 Opening

A few minutes to open the room

Set the tone — curiosity over fear, judgement over hype.

A short, opening intended to inspire: these tools don't shrink us, they expand us.

10 min
★ Warm-up · on our feet

Where do we stand?

Get us moving — and see the room honestly.

A few statements, and everyone moves to where they stand.

5 min
01 Together · quiz

How wrong are we about AI?

Meet our own assumptions — and watch a few of them fall.

A playful quiz on the things we're sure we know. The point isn't to be right — it's to notice how confidently we can be wrong.

10 min
02 Hands-on · teams

Prompt battle

Discover that the prompt — not the tool — does the work.

Same challenge for every team, five minutes to craft the best prompt, then we run them live and the funniest result wins.

15 min
03 Together · build it up

Make the prompt better, together

Learn the anatomy of a good prompt — live.

We take one prompt and grow it together, layer by layer. — Each time we run it again, the answer sharpens right in front of us.

15 min
04 Hands-on · exploratory work

Spot the cracks

See how confident — and how wrong — AI can be at once.

First we hunt for the errors: confident answer — a false figure, an invented source. Then we turn the tables and try to make the model fail: contradict itself, miscount, stumble.

15 min
05 Framing · inspiration

Use cases

See the breadth — then find what fits your week.

A quick tour of the everyday ways AI helps.

10 min
06 Together · your work

A recurring task, solved

Make it personal — and immediate.

Everyone names one recurring, time-eating task from their own week. We pick a few and work through them together — so people leave with something real, not hypothetical.

20 min
07 Together · end to end

From data to a presentation

The whole journey: messy input → insight → something we can share.

We take a dataset, let AI help us make sense of it, and turn it into something clear enough to present.

20 min
Approximate running time ≈ 2 hours · timings are a working draft, adjustable to the slot.
